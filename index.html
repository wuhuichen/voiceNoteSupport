Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I record a voice memo?

    Open the app and tap the "Record" button. Speak clearly into your device's microphone. Tap "Stop" when you're done.

  • How do I transcribe a recording?

    After recording, the app will automatically transcribe your voice memo into text. You can view the transcription in the app.

  • How do I summarize a transcription?

    Select the transcription you want to summarize and tap the "Summarize" button. The app will generate a concise summary using AI.

  • How do I access my saved recordings and transcriptions?

    Go to the "Records" tab to view all your saved recordings and transcriptions.

  • How do I subscribe for unlimited recording time?

    Go to the "User Info" tab and follow the subscription instructions to unlock unlimited recording time.
